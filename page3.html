About Us

We are a group of Ethiopian professionals with diverse skills and experiences in Telecommunications, Operator Business, Enterprise Networking, Information Security, Software Development, Business Leadership and Strategy after long stints working in different parts of the world who came together to form Abren Technologies - which is where the name Abren came from which means together in Amharic (Ethiopia's popular language).

We started Abren Technologies in 2020 with a vision to positively impact the current ongoing digital transformation in Ethiopia.
